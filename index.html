Introducing Ze Wei and his sister




Having learnt the piano and violin since the age of 4, my years in Dunman High really exposed me to plenty of music, and this exposure (in String Ensemble and Music Elective Programme), together with the invaluable advice from my piano teacher, has enabled me to obtain the DipABRSM and Licentiate of the Royal Schools of Music (LRSM) with Distinction for piano performance certificates in Year 1 and 3 respectively. I am also humbled to have been awarded the MOE - MEP Scholarship earlier this year.

As I wrap up my pursuit of music examination certificates at the end of this year, 2015 is the time to share and spread my passion for music with the people closest to my heart, before the A levels kick in next year, and before the grueling National Service the year after.

I am truly glad to share the stage on 16th December with my sister, Wen Yan of 4J, who like me has a strong passion for music. She has also attained her DipABRSM for Piano Performance.
